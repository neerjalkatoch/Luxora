Privacy Policy - Luxora

Introduction

At Luxora, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, services, or communications.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full Name, Email Address, Phone Number
  • Business Information: Company Name, Website URL, Industry Details
  • Technical Data: IP Address, Browser, Device Info
  • Communication Data: Emails, consultations, project discussions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, deliver services, improve user experience, analyze performance, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance website functionality and marketing performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted service providers or authorities when required.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for services and compliance.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, but no system is completely secure.

7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country with proper safeguards.

8. Your Rights

You can access, update, delete, or restrict your personal data.

9. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for external websites.

10. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically.

12. Contact

Contact us through your website for any privacy-related queries.
